What is nUSD? Nexxore Native
nUSD is Nexxore's native stablecoin, minted by depositing collateral (ETH, WBTC, stablecoins). Unlike traditional stables, your collateral remains productive—earning yield across DeFi protocols while backing your nUSD.

Key Benefits:
• Capital Efficiency — Your collateral generates yield instead of sitting idle
• On-Chain Transparency — All positions tracked in real-time on-chain
• Risk-Adjusted — Collateral ratios vary by strategy risk profile
• Auto-Liquidation Protection — Smart rebalancing prevents liquidations
