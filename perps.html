How Funding Works

Funding keeps prices aligned. When too many longs exist, longs pay shorts. When too many shorts exist, shorts pay longs. This creates a self-balancing system without protocol risk.

Collateral Protection

Your collateral caps your maximum loss. Positions are automatically liquidated when health factor drops to 1.0, protecting both you and the LP vault from bad debt.

LP Yield Sources

LPs earn from three sources: trading fees (0.05%), funding rate imbalances, and liquidation penalties. Yields are variable and market-dependent.

Modular Architecture

Every component is a separate contract: PerpMarket, FundingEngine, MarginVault, LiquidationEngine. This ensures security, upgradability, and auditability.

System Architecture

NX-Perps is built with a fully modular, on-chain architecture. No CEX-style matching engine. Every position is deterministic and verifiable.

PerpMarket
Manages positions, tracks open interest, and coordinates trading. One contract per market pair (ETH-USD, BTC-USD, etc.).
FundingEngine
Stateless calculator that computes funding rates based on OI imbalance. Updates funding index which positions accumulate against.
MarginVault
Holds all collateral tokens. Enforces balances and margin requirements. Completely isolated from directional risk.
LiquidationEngine
Monitors health factors and executes partial/full liquidations. Pays incentives to liquidator bots to ensure system safety.
LP Vaults
Market-specific liquidity pools that provide capital backstop. Earn fees and funding, absorb extreme PnL events.
Factory
Deploys new markets, sets risk parameters, registers vaults. Ensures consistent deployment and configuration.
